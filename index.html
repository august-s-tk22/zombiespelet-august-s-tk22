How to play:

Use the W, A, S, D keys to navigate the player.

Use the left and right arrow keys to fire guns, and space to use the baseball bat (melee weapon).

Press G to drop the weapon currently held.

If your health gets too low, a medkit will spawn. Colliding with it will restore your health to its entirety.

You start the game with only the baseball bat. At 500 points, the handheld gun will spawn. At 1500 points, the rifle will spawn.

The game is meant to be played in fullscreen. Press F11 on Windows, or CMD + Shift + F on Mac.
