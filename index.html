How to play:

Use the W, A, S, D keys to navigate the player.

Use the left and right arrow keys to fire guns.

Use the spacebar to attack with the bat (melee weapon).

Press G to drop the weapon currently held.

If your health gets too low, a medkit will spawn. Colliding with it will restore your health to full.

The handheld gun spawns at 500 points, and the assault rifle spawns at 2000 points. After that, see how long you can last before dying!
